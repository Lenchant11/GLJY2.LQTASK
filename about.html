About Liqun Task

Empowering Brands with Real User Feedback Worldwide

Liqun Task is a forward-thinking user research and product testing platform committed to helping global companies enhance their digital experiences. By connecting real users with top brands, we facilitate meaningful feedback that drives product innovation and customer satisfaction.

Founded with the vision of democratizing research participation, Liqun Task enables everyday individuals to take part in usability studies, beta testing, and micro-tasks — all from the convenience of their devices. Participants are fairly compensated for their time and effort, receiving digital rewards such as gift cards upon successful task completion.

Our Mission

We strive to make product development more user-centric by bridging the gap between businesses and authentic customer experiences. Whether it's testing a new mobile app, reviewing a website interface, or answering targeted surveys, every task performed helps companies refine their offerings and better serve their audiences.

Global Access

With a remote-friendly approach, Liqun Task welcomes participants from around the world. No prior experience is required — just honest opinions, a stable internet connection, and a willingness to contribute to product improvement.

Trusted by Innovators

Our platform is used by emerging startups and established enterprises alike. We maintain strict privacy and ethical standards to ensure a safe, transparent environment for all participants and clients.
