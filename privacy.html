Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us at Liqun Task

This Privacy Policy outlines how Liqun Task collects, uses, and protects any information provided by users participating in our research programs.

Information We Collect

Liqun Task does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) unless it is voluntarily submitted by the user, such as through contact forms or feedback submissions. We do not request sensitive data like identification numbers, banking information, or passwords.

How We Use Information

Any information submitted by participants (e.g., email address, feedback responses) is used solely to:

  • Facilitate communication and reward delivery
  • Improve the quality of our research and services
  • Understand user experience and task performance

Data Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. Liqun Task uses industry-standard security protocols to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure.

Third-Party Tools

We may use third-party analytics or messaging tools (e.g., Google Analytics, WhatsApp) to improve our service experience. These tools may collect limited, non-identifiable data such as browser type or device model.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance browsing experience. Users can control cookie behavior via their browser settings at any time.

External Links

Our platform may contain links to external websites. Liqun Task is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third-party sites.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. Users are encouraged to review this page regularly to stay informed about how we protect their data.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at support@liquntask.com.
